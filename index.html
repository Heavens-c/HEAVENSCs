About Me

Hi, I'm [Heavensc], a passionate script developer specializing in creating immersive experiences for the FiveM modification of a popular open-world game. My journey began in 2019, and since then, I have dedicated myself to crafting custom scripts and environments that enhance multiplayer gaming.

I have worked on developing incredible cities such as Billionaire City, Underworld, City of Dreams, PH Gov City, and Density. Each of these projects has challenged and refined my skills, from scripting intricate systems to ensuring seamless multiplayer integration.

My contributions extend to frameworks like QBCORE, where a small piece of my code has been treasured as a valuable contribution. I am driven by a passion for learning and have experience in PHP (basic), Lua (moderate), and database management with MySQL and MariaDB. I'm also exploring C, C++, Python, and soon, Rust.
